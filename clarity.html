Clarity

Better Data for Patients

Empower patients through their data

Whether you are a patient or a physician you need access to consistent, high quality data. Clarity® is an intuitive mobile application designed to improve the quality of data to assist in medical care decisions. Used with or without linkage to Acuity®, this application captures repeated measures of patient health while establishing a secure mobile medical record for the patient.

Connect patients and care providers

For care teams, Clarity serves as your virtual clinic assistant. Our system integrates with your hospital information systems to check in patients and provide clear, concise summaries of patient problems, medications and self-reported health.

Designed to augment existing ambulatory care systems, Clarity works seamlessly to correlate changes in patient health status with diagnostic test results to assist medical decision making. Administrators can monitor clinic volumes, referral indications and patient demographics in real time. Researchers can approach and consent eligible patients for local registries or trials through our fully automated consent process. Automated and secure linkage between Clarity® and Acuity® allows physicians to immediately access diagnostic test results and view them with the patient, aided by our intuitive and patient-friendly graphical report summaries.

This is all accomplished within a secure, HIPAA compliant cloud-based architecture managed by a sophisticated Patient Identified Data Service (PIDS®). PIDS® ensures that patient identifiers can only be associated with our cloud-hosted anonymous data while within a secure institutional network.
