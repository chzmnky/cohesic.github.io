HOW CAN WE USE PATIENT DATA TO IMPROVE CLINICAL DECISIONS?

Today's healthcare system is complex. Institutional information systems are rapidly being challenged by expanding demands to support and manage medical diagnostic and patient-health data. Cohesic is focused on bridging the expanding gap between legacy system capacity and future need for the delivery of data-driven, individualized patient care.

At Cohesic, we are changing the way data is generated and used in healthcare. Through intuitive cloud-based applications, we are creating a cohesive system for the capture and meaningful use of data in patient care. Our software allows diagnostic test providers to increase efficiency and data quality, referring physicians to make data-supported decisions, and patients to engage in their care.

Acuity - Better Data For Care Providers

Acuity® is an intuitive suite of applications designed for the seamless integration of diagnostic imaging teams and their workflow. From incoming electronic referrals to automated protocolling, patient consent, standardized interpretation, and report distribution: we manage it all.

Using our secure, cloud-based architecture, we integrate with your hospital information system to track all data needed to improve service quality and value. We provide the most elegant natural language generation (NLG) and graphical reporting interfaces developed to date, improving ease and quality of test interpretations while delivering meaningful and interactive reports to referring physicians and patients.

Clarity - Better Data For Patients

Clarity® is an intuitive mobile app designed to allow patients to engage in their medical care. Used with or without Acuity®, this app captures relevant and repeated measures of patient health, both at clinic visits and in everyday life. With patient-friendly graphical displays of test results and health trends, patients become active participant and feel more engaged in their care.

Clarity® provides a partnered tablet-based app for care providers that displays longitudinal measures of patient-reported health with diagnostic test results supported by Acuity®. This unique combination of data can improve clinical decision making and establishes our novel paradigm for the delivery of individualized patient care.
