We build clinical workflow, diagnostic interpretation and patient participation software for cardiology. 

Patients First

To improve healthcare for patients with cardiovascular disease, both patients and providers require the right tools for the job. Healthcare IT departments need enterprise-wide solutions to manage massive amounts of data, keep patient information safe, and improve access to patient records. We create tools designed specifically for cardiovascular patients and their providers that use existing IT systems to facilitate the best possible care.

Step 1: Calibrate

In the past, much of the data collected in healthcare focused on capturing just enough. Our customers feel as if they are living in a data void and strongly believe that by capturing better data today, they will be able to make better decisions tomorrow.

People In Context

Electronic Medical Records are great but historical records don't tell you how a patient is feeling today. Patients, Doctors, Clerks, Techs and Nurses use our tools to capture the information needed to put the patient in context for the entire cardiology healthcare team.

Standardized Interpretation

Interpreting diagnostic tests is really hard to do and being the best in the world is even harder. Our tools combine societal guidelines with world-leading expertise to standardize diagnostic reports and help everyone do the best possible job in the least amount of time.

Better Communication

When providers and patients receive written diagnostic reports in a consistent format every time, they understand the results better. This improved understanding leads to more confident and informed decision making for both patients and providers.

Step 2: Learn

We extend our customers' quality assurance (QA), quality improvement (QI), business intelligence and analytics programs to address the specific needs of Cardiology.

Business Intelligence

Most care programs admit their team struggles to find the time required for continuous improvement. We work with the world's leading cardiovascular programs to extract their best practices and benchmarks and bring them to every customer.

Machine Inteligence

We believe there are some things machines can do better than humans and visa versa. We do not believe in replacing humans, rather we seek to augment humans by providing new machine intelligence tools.

Give Feedback

Change is hard but, in the absence of good feedback, it's almost impossible. We close the loop on patient care and let everyone know how their decisions affect patient outcomes.

Step 3: Change

With better data, smarter tools, and continuous feedback, our customers can effect change and focus on positively impacting patient outcomes.

Better Automation

Providers are spending obtuse amounts of their precious time manually entering data for QA and QI purposes. We automate QA and QI and make everyone happy.

Better Decisions

Providers and patients must make decisions based on a plethora of diagnostic test results, trends and first-hand knowledge. We extend and enhance the information available in cardiovascular care so that everyone may make an informed decision.

Better Outcomes

This is what we are all working towards and we know that it is possible. Every tool we build with our customers and their patients is one more step towards improving outcomes for patients with cardiovascular disease.

That's our Why.
