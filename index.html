Better care through better data

Is patient data being used to make better decisions?

Today, clinicians know very little about the patient they are trying to help. Diagnostic test providers intepret results without the context given by patient data. Patients do not have access to their own data and their day-to-day health is not unmeasured and unobserved by their care team.

At Cohesic, we are changing the way data is generated and used in healthcare. Through cloud-based applications, we are creating a cohesive system for health data, allowing providers to give better care, clinicians to make better decisions and patients to be in control of their data.

Learn more

Acuity - Better Data For Providers

Unstandardized practices for diagnostic reporting prevent clinicians from seeing a cohesive picture of patient health and assessing quality of care. Our flagship product Acuity is a novel diagnostic reporting platform that replicates the freedom of free-form dictation through natural language generation while elevating data quality and depth for patient phenotyping.

Learn more

Clarity - Better Data For Patients

Your health determines your quality of life, but your care team only sees it in snapshots, usually when you have an appointment. What happens between those visits? Our mobile app Clarity allows you to tell your care team about your health today so that they can help you better tomorrow.
