Better Data for Care Providers

Diagnostic testing is a rapidly changing and expanding part of healthcare. Departments are under rising pressures to achieve standardization and quality assurance despite limited resources for data capture and reporting. The team at Cohesic believes that a single, cloud-based solution integrated with the existing hospital information system can solve these challenges.

You need Acuity速

Acuity速 is an intuitive software platform designed for seamless integration of diagnostic testing workflow. Managing incoming referrals, protocolling, triaging, booking, consent, standardized reporting and report distribution; it is now all managed within a single, cloud-based environment. This allows all data relevant to your practice to be at your fingertips anytime, anywhere.

When partnered with Clarity速, our patient-health application, patients complete pre-procedural documentation prior to arrival, receive notifications of booking changes, provide consent for procedures and data sharing, and complete patient satisfaction surveys over their own mobile device. Overall, Cohesic is changing the way Diagnostic facilities operate, and is delivering a new level of engagement between physicians and patients.
