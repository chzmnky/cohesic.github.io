Better Data for Care Providers

To deliver personalized care...

Diagnostic testing is core to today’s medical practices and is essential to the delivery of individualized patient care. Rising pressures for standardization, quality, and value are being matched by immediate desires to improve clinician-clinician communication and engage patients in their care. However, reliance on existing systems to accomplish these tasks often seems futile. The team at Cohesic team believes that a single, cloud-based solution that integrates with existing hospital information systems can solve these challenges.

You need Acuity®

Acuity® is an intuitive app-based platform designed for seamless integration of the diagnostic imaging team and their workflow. From incoming diagnostic test referrals to protocolling, triaging / booking, patient check-in and consent, standardized interpretation and report distribution; we manage it all within a single, secure cloud-based environment. This allows all data relevant to your practice to be at your fingertips anytime, anywhere.

When partnered with Clarity®, our patient-care mobile App, patients complete all pre-procedural documentation prior to arrival, receive notifications of booking changes, provide consent for procedures and data sharing, and complete post-procedure patient satisfaction surveys over their own mobile device. Overall, Cohesic is providing a unique opportunity for diagnostic testing facilities to truly understand their patients and their practice.
